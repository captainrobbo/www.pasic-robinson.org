Lima, Peru

In late January we set off for a tour of Latin America. We flew into Sao Paolo, then on to Lima, Cusco and Macchu Picchu and Arequipa in Peru; Santiago in Chile; then across the Amdes from Puerto Montt to Bariloche in Argentina; then Mendoza, Buenos Aires and back via Sao Paolo! This page just covers Lima - later stages are in the side menu.
