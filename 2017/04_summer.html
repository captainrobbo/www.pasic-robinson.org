Long summer holiday

We kicked off by flying to Mirna's 60th birthday in a ski lodge above Mostar, Bosnia, followed by a few days on the beach, then back home to catch up on work and see Harry off on his Interrailing. A work engagement in Tuscany (honest!) provided an opportunity to take it slowly by car, driving through France and Mont Blance to Italy after a few of Tuscan scenery in scorching heat. We then crossed over to Croatia on a ferry. This time we had long enough to see Bosnia and spend time in Mostar and Sarajevo. On the way back we saw sights on the Rhine in Germany.
