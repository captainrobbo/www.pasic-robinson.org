Skiing - New Year and February

We saw in the New Year in the Alps, where the British team at Les Hautes Auguilles had its usual superb feast. Sadly there was not much snow on our side, so we went to the Italian side a couple of times, and explored Swiss hot baths. Our last night was in Evian where they have an amazing driftwood show, Les Flottins. We came back in February and were rewarded with lots of snow and blue skies.
