Greece

Our usual Croatian summer wasn't going to happen, so we took a week's package to Kalamata in Greece in August. It's a novely spending a week in a nice hotel in August, rather than fixing up the family beach house and doing it all ourselves.

We had two excursions - one driving down into the wild and historic Mani, and one to ancient Messini, the best preserved ruins in Greece, just down the road
