Tim's 10th birthday

Thailand, 21 Feb 2007

Tim's birthday fell during our holiday in Thailand. In fact he chose the location - they had so much fun the year before at the Holiday Inn kids' club that he insisted on going back and we booked our last 4 days there. His favourite teacher from school, Miss Ralph, now works in Thailand and she came to see us for the day. The hotel laid on a great party for us and some new friends...
