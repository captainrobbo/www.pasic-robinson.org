Croatia 2 - Conference

In September, Andy was co-chairing an international athletics conference in Zadar, a few hours up the Croatian coast from where we normally stay. People from most countries in Europe flew in, and it was the best yet in this conference series.

After a crazy week of non-stop work, we had a day in Split with the co-organiser, Nicolas, and his wife Samantha; and then met Elif and Mel to unwind and show them around.
