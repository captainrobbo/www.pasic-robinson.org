Japan

Without a doubt our big holiday of the year was our trip to Japan in October. Andy used to live there and had been going on about how great it was for years, so it was time to find out. Aided by two families of former neighbours from Wimbledon who have since moved back to Japan, we had a fantastic combination of history, scenery and high-tech fun (much of it involving their toilets, which do things I will not describe on this site but which fascinated the boys!).
